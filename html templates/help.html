Help

Create a task

To create a task please click at the "Add Task" button you find at the navigation bar to your left. There you enter all data related to your task and set those Members assigned to your task. By clicking the blue Button "Add Task" your created task will be put to the Backlog.

Backlog

You can enter the backlog area by clicking the link in the navigation bar to your left. There you can either controll and edit your tasks, as well deleting them or pushing them to the board by clicking on the pin button at the right.

Board

Use drag and drop to move tasks between the diffrent columns depending on the status of the tasks. To delete a task you can drop it at the red marked area at the bottom.
